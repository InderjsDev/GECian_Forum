  • Basics

  • What is the Shop option provided in app?

    Shop option takes to the shop feed(s).These shop feed(s) can be posted by student or teachers to sell old or new education related material/stuff to other users of the app.

  • Is posting shop feed(s) Free of cost?

    By default everything on the GECian app is free of cost. However posting shop feed(s) per user have limit upto 5 only

  • Can I remove a post?

    Yes. You can remove the posts or events that are created by you only.

  • Can I login by Google using same email address that i used for normal sign up ?

    Yes.

  • Account

  • How do I change my password?

    If you are not logged In, you can enter your email address in field and can tap forgot password in order to change old one. If you are logged In, you have to log out to do the same.

  • How do I delete my account?

    Deleting account is not available yet. We will try our best to add this feature in next updates.

  • How do I change my account settings?

    You can change your personal details in the navigation view in left sliding menu of the app.

  • I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

    If you are not logged In, you can enter your email address in field and can tap forgot password in order to change old one. If you are logged In, you have to log out to do the same.

  • Posts

  • Can I post unlimited posts?

    Yes, you can post unlimited posts in news feed and can create unlimited event because only particular number of latest posts remains on the server.

  • How do I delete my shop posts?

    Tap three dots button in the upper right corner of the app and press my posts. Then select particular posts to delete and delete them by tapping three dots button of individual posts.

  • I'm getting Notifications for every single post. How to stop them all?

    Go to settings the advanced notification then select the notifications you want to mute.

  • Chats

  • Can I chat to everyone in the app?

    No. You can only talk to the person posted some shop post.

  • My previous chat messags are missing, how to get them back?

    For security purposes chat messages get deleted by own. However limited number of messages remains there untill you delete the chat.

  • How do I block someone in chats?

    Use block button provided in the top bar of chats.

  • Contact for support help

  • What should I do if my app getting crash and i can't reach support option in settings?

    simply mail us on : "helpgecian@gmail.com" with subject "Crash report"

  • This post is abusive or illegal or not good for me. What should i do?

    You can report the post with three dots menu provided in every single post. GECian team will decide whether to keep that post or delete, it would be based on the content of post.

  • I need to get the information about something related to GEC college.

    We don't have/provide current information to any individual about the college. For ex. last date to fill some form in college.
